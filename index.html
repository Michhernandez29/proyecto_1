Phil Oh’s Best Street Style Photos From Paris Fashion Week Fall 2022

Paris Fashion Week street style started off with guests attending a tribute to Off-White’s Virgil Abloh and will conclude with showgoers outside Miu Miu’s fall 2022 runway.In between, editors, retailers, models, and more will take in Parisian classics like Christian Dior, Balenciaga, Saint Laurent, and Chanel as well as a new generation that spans Vaquera to Rokh. Vogue’s Phil Oh will be braving the elements on the street to capture all the best outfits from the City of Light.
